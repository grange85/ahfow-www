<p><a href="/database/dean-and-britta/dean-and-britta-releases/dean-and-britta-mistress-america.html">Dean Wareham and Britta Phillips' soundtrack</a> for the upcoming Noah Baumbach film <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt2872462/">Mistress America</a> will be released by <a href="http://milanrecords.com/">Milan Records</a> in August when the film gets it's general release.</p>
<p>Dean & Britta previously scored Baumbach's <a href="/database/dean-and-britta/dean-and-britta-releases/dean-and-britta-the-squid-and-the-whale.html">The Squid and the Whale</a>, and Dean has been involved in most of his films in some form or other.</p>
