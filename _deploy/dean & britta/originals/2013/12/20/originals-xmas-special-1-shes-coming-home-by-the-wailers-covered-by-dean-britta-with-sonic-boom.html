Originals: She's Coming Home by The Wailers (covered by Dean & Britta with Sonic Boom): Xmas special #3

Dec 20, 2013 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@fullofwishes.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

Nope! Not those Wailers, these Wailers were from Tacoma Washington and one of their biggest fans (according to Wikipedia) was a young Jimi Hendrix. They recorded She's Coming Home for a Christmas album they shared with fellow garage bands The Sonics and The Galaxies (weird... Sonics (like Sonic Boom) and Galaxies like in 500!) in 1965

Dean & Britta's version was gender adjusted, recorded (again) with Sonic Boom and released on a single by Chimney Rocks! Records (the b-side of which has already put in an appearance on this list).

Dean & Britta with Sonic Boom - He's Coming Home

The whole of this splendid Christmas album is available on Spotify, and is worth the time (although judicious use of that skip button is advised).
