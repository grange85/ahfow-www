Issue 14 contains:
A Letter from Damon & Naomi
The Poet in my Heart, by Kim Gordon
time again to start again, by Jim O’Rourke
Fortune, by Naomi Yang
Goodbye, by Bill Knott
Postscript to no. 14, by the Editors
