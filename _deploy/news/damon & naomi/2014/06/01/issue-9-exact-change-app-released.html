Issue 9 of Damon & Naomi's Exact Change e-zine released

Jun 1, 2014 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@fullofwishes.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

The latest issue of Damon & Naomi's iOS e-zine Exact Change is now available from the App Store

The Exact Change app is monthly e-zine. The app is a free download, and includes the first issue free (if you poke around you’ll actually find three free issues in there, the additional two are easter eggs we snuck in from the 1980s). Additional issues are available by subscription for $2.99 a month, or $29.99 a year. Individual issues, and back issues, are $4.99.

Issue 9 includes:

The latest number of the Exact Change e-zine is out now - with David Hockney's iPad drawings of spring, Stephin Merritt's radical decorative borders (complete with soundtrack), and Rainer Maria Rilke's bizarre "Primal Sound"
