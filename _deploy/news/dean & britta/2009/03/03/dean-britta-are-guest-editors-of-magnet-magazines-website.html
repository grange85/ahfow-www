Dean & Britta are guest editors of Magnet Magazine's website

Mar 3, 2009 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@fullofwishes.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

Dean & Britta are this weeks guest editor at Magnet Magazine's website and their tenure kicked off yesterday with a Q&A interview during which he confirms that the last page of Black Postcards will be fixed for the paperback edition (my apologies for going on about just how much that bothered me!)

Are there any postscripts you’d like to add to Black Postcards?
Wareham: I have fixed a few things for the paperback edition (due April 28). Apparently, I had gotten the last song at the final Luna show wrong. That was because my set list that night said the last song was “Indian Summer.” I forgot that we had changed it during a last-minute discussion before the show to “23 Minutes in Brussels.”
