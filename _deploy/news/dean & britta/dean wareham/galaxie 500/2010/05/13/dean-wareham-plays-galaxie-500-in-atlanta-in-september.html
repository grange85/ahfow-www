Dean Wareham plays Galaxie 500 in Atlanta… in September

May 13, 2010 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@fullofwishes.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

A show billed as "Dean Wareham plays Galaxie 500" is taking place at The Earl in Atlanta, GA on the 17 September. I'd hazard a guess that this is not, as suggested on the promoter's MySpace page "the first time in 20 years" but similar to the Dean & Britta Galaxie 500 shows in New York, Castellon and Paris in the last couple of years. Still a not too common occurrence. Tickets are on sale now at $18 and given current Pitchfork love for Galaxie 500 and the fact that they have just plugged this show it might be worth getting your tickets in advance! - news </p>
