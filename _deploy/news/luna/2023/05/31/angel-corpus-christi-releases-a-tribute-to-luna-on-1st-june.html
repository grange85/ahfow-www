Angel Corpus Christi releases a 'tribute to Luna' on 1st June

May 31, 2023 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@grange85.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

The wonderful Angel Corpus Christi will be releasing Bewitched: A Tribute to Luna on 1st June on limited edition 12” vinyl, available on Angel’s Bandcamp, and in all the usual digital/streamed locations. The album contains covers of six Luna tracks including her cover of I Want Everything that previously came out on a single in 2001.

Buy Angel Corpus Christi - Bewitched: A Tribute to Luna for $12 + postage

Angel, of course, has some Luna/Dean Wareham form, as well as her previously mentioned cover of “I Want Everything”…

  • ACC dueted with Dean on Je t’aime/I Wanna Boogie With You
  • Dean & Britta played on ACC’s cover of Femme Fatale
  • Dean play guitar on You
  • Here’s Dean behind her on stage
  • And she’s been the sender of “strange faxes” to Dean
  • etc.
