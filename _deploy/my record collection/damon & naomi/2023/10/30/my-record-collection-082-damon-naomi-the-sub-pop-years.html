#082: Damon & Naomi - The Sub Pop Years

Oct 30, 2023 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@grange85.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

The Sub Pop Years was released in August 2009 and is a compilation of tracks from the albums Damon & Naomi released for Sub Pop starting with their second album The Wondrous World of Damon & Naomi and finishing with the “live” album Song to The Siren.

The CD came in a lovely gatefold card case with a fold-out insert. The insert has descriptions of each of the albums that made up the duo’s Sub Pop years and was, I think, the first time they openly admitted that Live in San Sebastian was in fact recorded live in their living room in Cambridge, MA (with a crowd dubbed on from a show in San Francisco).

The CD of The Sub Pop Years is a lovely package, with a wonderful track list and is still available to buy from D&N’s website - I’d very highly reccomend it - particularly if you haven’t bought anything since More Sad Hits (which the AHFoW annual survey always suggested was the case).

Obv also available for pay what you can on Bandcamp.
