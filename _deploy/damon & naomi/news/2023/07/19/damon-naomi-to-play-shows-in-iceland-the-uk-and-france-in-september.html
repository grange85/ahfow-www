Damon & Naomi to play shows in Iceland, the UK and France in September

Jul 19, 2023 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@grange85.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

Damon & Naomi are heading to Europe to play their first dates outside North America since 2019.

They will play their first ever date in Reykjavik, Iceland om the 1st September and will follow this with three dates in the UK and one in Paris.

1st September 2023 Mengi, Reykjavík, Iceland
Damon & Naomi
3rd September 2023 Cafe OTO, London, UK
Damon & Naomi
4th September 2023 Gullivers, Manchester, UK
Damon & Naomi
5th September 2023 Glad Cafe, Glasgow, UK
Damon & Naomi
9th September 2023 Cafe de Paris, Paris, France
Damon & Naomi

For the dates in London and Glasgow support will come from Richard Youngs.
