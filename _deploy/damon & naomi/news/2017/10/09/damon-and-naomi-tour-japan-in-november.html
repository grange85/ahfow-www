Damon & Naomi are heading to Japan for a few dates in November playing with Michio Kurihara and with support from Ghost's Masaki Batoh.

The shows are supporting the recent Japanese CD release of a live album Everything Quieter than Everything Else. The album is made up from two shows in Japan in 2005 and 2008.

12 October 2017 The Root Cellar, Greenfield, MA, USA
6 November 2017 Namba Bears, Osaka, Japan
7 November 2017 Jittoku, Kyoto, Japan
10 November 2017 Shibuya - 7th Floor, Tokyo, Japan
11 November 2017 Tokorozawa - Mojo, Saitama, Japan
