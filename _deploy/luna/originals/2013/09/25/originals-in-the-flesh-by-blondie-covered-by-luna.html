Blondie - In The Flesh (1976)

Blondie's In The Flesh was their second single released in October 1976. It reached number 2 in the Australian charts making it Blondie's first hit record.

Luna's version was released as a 7" single on Trade2 Records, and later on the CD single of Bobby Peru.
