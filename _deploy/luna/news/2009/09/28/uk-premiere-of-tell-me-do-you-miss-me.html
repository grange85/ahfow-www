The UK premiere of Matthew Buzzell's film Tell Me Do You Miss Me, which documents the final months of Luna, will take place on Sunday 25 October at The Half Moon in Herne Hill, London. The showing is at 7pm and will be followed by "The Dream Machine all-stars playing a set of rare Luna songs".

Entrance is free and to fill your time (and empty your pockets) before the show you can check out the Dream Machine Record Fair which will be on at the venue all day from noon.
