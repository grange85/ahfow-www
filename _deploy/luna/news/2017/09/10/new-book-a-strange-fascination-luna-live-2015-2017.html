Book: A Strange Fascination - Luna - live 2015 - 2017

Sep 10, 2017 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@grange85.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

Over the last couple of years A Head Full of Wishes has been lucky (and honoured) to have had the opportunity to publish a lot of Joakim Bengtsson's diairies as he and Ulrika followed Luna around the world. Now he ane Ulrika have collected all their writings (many of which haven't been published here!) into a beautiful book - A Strange Fascination: Luna live 2015 - 2017.

'A Strange Fascination' is a book about when Luna reunited in 2015. It's a collection of travel diaries from the 29 concerts we have been to during 2015–2017. The book contains previously published and unpublished texts and many pictures of the band and their fans. It will be released in September 2017 and will be limited to 350 copies.
A Strange Fascination website

The book is 340 pages with lots of photos and is available to order now for either $15 if you're able to get it from them in person at one of the shows they're attending in the coming months or $25 including postage anywhere in the world.

All the information is available on the book webpage - and you can also follow on Facebook and Instagram.
