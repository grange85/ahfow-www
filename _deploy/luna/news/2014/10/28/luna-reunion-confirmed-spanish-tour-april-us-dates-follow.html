Luna reunion confirmed: Spanish tour in April with US dates to follow

Oct 28, 2014 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@fullofwishes.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

Luna have now confirmed that they are reunitiung for a tour of Spain in April 2015, and Dean confirmed on Twitter that there would be "a few US dates" later in the year.

Confirmed dates are as follows:

  • 17 April 2015 - Gijón Sound Festival, Gijón
  • 18 April 2015 - Escenario Santander, Santander
  • 20 April 2015 - Teatro Lara, Madrid
  • 21 April 2015 - Aulario la Bomba, Cádiz
  • 22 April 2015 - La Rambleta, Valencia
  • 23 April 2015 - Bikini, Barcelona
  • 24 April 2015 - Centro de Las Armas, Zaragoza
  • 25 April 2015 - Intxaurrondo, San Sebastián
