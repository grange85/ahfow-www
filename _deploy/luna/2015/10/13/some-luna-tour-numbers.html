Oh look! LUNA have a night off... what the hell will I do with my time!?

Luckily Michael emailed me this morning with some stats for what Luna had played over the three dates in NYC - "interesting", I thought, "but if you've done the hard work..." - so I decided to extend it to the whole of the US tour so far. People with a life should probably leave now... oh! they already have... so it's just me and you then... here we go...

  • Luna have played nine shows since they started the US tour in Atlanta on the 2 October.
  • They have played 35 different songs
  • They have played five songs at all nine shows
  • They have plated the whole of Penthouse (obviously - they did that on night one!)
  • They have played nothing from The Days of Our Nights or Romnatica
  • They have played two Galaxie 500 songs
