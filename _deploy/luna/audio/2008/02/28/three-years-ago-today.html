Three years ago today...

Feb 28, 2008 • {"display_name"=>"Andy", "login"=>"admin", "email"=>"andy@fullofwishes.co.uk", "url"=>"http://www.grange85.co.uk"}

Now I know that three years isn’t one of those meaningful anniversaries but it popped into my head that today was the third anniversary of Luna’s last show - so here’s the the final two songs of the last show - you can hear the emotion through the cracked voices and fluffed notes..

Indian Summer - Luna (28th February 2005)

Source: 28th February 2005 - Luna - Bowery Ballroom, New York NY, USA

23 Minutes in Brussels - Luna (28th February 2005)

Source: 28th February 2005 - Luna - Bowery Ballroom, New York NY, USA

If wallowing in nostalgia is your thing then also check out the Luna’s Last Waltz group blog that includes contributions from both Dean & Britta. And there’s also stacks of pictures to browse through…
