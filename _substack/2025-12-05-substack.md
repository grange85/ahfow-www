### Damon & Naomi - Attention Shoppers (London, 2001)

I don't have many recordings of Damon & Naomi but this recording was made by my taping pal Andy who recorded quite a few shows with/for me around that time, I gave the recording its name because at the beginning of Damon & Naomi's set Damon calls out _"attention shoppers"_ in an attempt to attract the attention of the noisy patrons at the bar.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/damon-and-naomi-attention-shoppers-cdr.jpg" "Damon & Naomi - Attention Shoppers" %}

This was a great show, support had come from The Clientele (first or second time I'd seen them) and The Trembling Blue Stars (sadly neither of their sets were taped). Damon & Naomi were over with Michio Kurihara on the tour that would end with the "Live in San Sebastian" deception (go read your Sub Pop Years liner notes).

I didn't often bother making sleeves for the recordings I have, but I did for this one. The show dates from a good ten years before my first phone with a camera and since I rarely took a _proper_ camera to shows the picture I used for the cover was not one of mine. A couple of weeks after the London show D&N played in Utrecht in Holland and the pictures from that show by the photographer Stefan Claudius were sent to me to include on AHFoW. I didn't get permission to bung it on my CD sleeve but then it was never meant for sharing. Stefan's photos also turned up in the "Song to the Siren" CD booklet.

The sleeve is obviously a riff on the sleeve of the recently released [Damon & Naomi with Ghost](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2023/10/02/my-record-collection-074-damon-naomi-with-ghost-cd/?utm_source=substack&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+20251205) which also seems to have been the inspiration, ten years later, for the Great Pop Supplement [Walking Backwards single](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2025/02/13/my-record-collection-damon-naomi-walking-backwards-worlds-strongest/?utm_source=substack&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+20251205).

I eventually did share the recording, and the sleeve, a few years later... and now I've just popped it on YouTube...

{% ahfowvideo "kHTxTyEsPes" "Damon & Naomi - Attention Shoppers (audio only, London 2001)" "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/damon-and-naomi-attention-shoppers-vid.jpg" %}

Listening back now I'm rather taken with how much more boisterous the audience seem to be than they have been at more recent Damon & Naomi shows - is that because we're all (band and audience) now 24 years older? It really was a cracking show and I'm glad I dragged Andy and his DAT machine along!

Some video from this show turned up on the DVD that was released with Song to The Siren in 2002:

{% ahfowvideo "aqfzVPcQlwQ" "Damon & Naomi in London 2001 (tour diary DVD)" "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/damon-and-naomi-song-to-the-siren-screengrab-01.jpg" %}

### The Wondrous World of Damon & Naomi (LP)

This is the Sub Pop LP release of Damon & Naomi's second album The Wondrous World of Damon & Naomi. I originally bought this on CD around the time of release and bought this LP in 2013 on Discogs for the collection.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/damon-and-naomi-wondrous-world-og-lp.jpg" "The Wondrous World of Damon & Naomi (LP)" %}

I loved Wondrous World on release and never stopped loving it (which is I think why I loved [the "bootleg" edition](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2025/06/23/my-record-collection-the-wondrous-world-of-damon-naomi-bootleg-edition/?utm_source=substack&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+20251205) slightly less).

I know I've said in the last couple of posts about Damon & Naomi albums that I find it impossible to pick a favourite but, for the annual AHFoW survey that ran from 1996 until 2020, I was obliged to pick an album. For most of those years it was _Wondrous World..._... except for the years it was _... with Ghost_.

While it is lovely to have the album in a 12" package it doesn't have the beautiful collage artwork that Naomi used for the CD booklet... so, swings and roundabouts.
