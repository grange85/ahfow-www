### My record collection [341] Galaxie 500 - CBGB 13th December 1988 (black vinyl LP)

Back at the beginning of the year Damon & Naomi played three genuinely sensational shows at Cafe OTO in London ([I wrote about them here](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2026/01/07/three-nights-with-damon-and-naomi-in-london/?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-10) but it deserves a re-write), this copy of Galaxie 500's recently released live LP was acquired at the second of those shows.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/galaxie-500-cbgb-black-vinyl.jpg" "Galaxie 500 - CBGB 13th December 1988 (black LP)" %}

After the show was finished I was loitering by the merch desk, chatting with Naomi and lending my pen out for signings. I mentioned to Naomi that [the "ultra clear and black cloudy" vinyl copy](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2025/08/14/my-record-collection-galaxie-500-cbgb-13th-december-1988/?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-10) I had bought on release was horrid and looked like a five year old had scribbled on it&dagger; and said I wanted to buy one of the black vinyl copies they were selling at the show. 

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/galaxie-500-cbgb-13th-december-1988-vinyl-mess.jpg" "records should never be this ugly" %}

Naomi insisted on giving me a copy.

OK, it doesn't come with a poster, and a single, and doesn't come in a fancy fat, card sleeve (that's too big) but it looks so much better sitting on my record player.

I love the photo by [Macioce](https://www.instagram.com/maciocephotography/) used on the cover - a couple of years ago Macioce was kind enough to send me an 11x14" print of the photo which is hanging in _"the museum"_.

 - *Catalogue Number:* AHFOW 12/112
 - *Artist:* Galaxie 500
 - *Title:* CBGB 13th December 1988
 - *Notes:* Black vinyl copy
 - *Format:* LP
 - [Buy 'CBGB 13th December 1988' on Bandcamp](https://galaxie500.bandcamp.com/album/cbgb-121388)

Previously in [my record collection](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/category/my-record-collection?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-10):
 - [[276] Galaxie 500 - CBGB - 13th December 1988](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2025/08/14/my-record-collection-galaxie-500-cbgb-13th-december-1988/?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-10)

_&dagger; - to be honest too many of these mixed colour, splatter, splash, burst, swirl, vinyl releases are horrible... it's time to stop and stick to single colour vinyl... and maybe more in black... I mean. when did black stop being the coolest colour?_

### My record collection [342] The 6ths - Wasps'' Nest (LP)

On first release in 1995 the only vinyl release of Wasps' Nest was a box set containing four seven inch EPs with all the tracks (and one bonus one) across them. Mean-ness and my full embrace of CD media meant that I didn't buy that but bought the [CD that I've already posted about](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2025/07/17/my-record-collection-the-6ths-wasps-nest-cd/?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-10), In 2017 Captured Tracks released the album on an LP - this is that.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/the-6ths-wasps-nest-lp.jpg" "The 6ths - Wasps' Nest" %}

The LP came in white vinyl and sadly didn't include the extra track it did howver have a larger booklet than the CD which meant that it could include the artist self-portraits that had been included in the box set but were missing from the CD package. Some were lovely and detailed, some were realistic, some were cartoon-y, some minimalist, some impressionist... and then there was Dean's:

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/the-6ths-wasps-nest-dean-wareham-portrait.jpg" "Dean Wareham self-portrait" %}

I think I said before how much I love this album and it really is a very beautiful thing, and doesn't have a dud on it!

 - *Catalogue Number:* AHFOW 12/073
 - *Artist:* The 6ths
 - *Title:* Wasps' Nest
 - *Notes:* White vinyl. Includes an 8" square booklet
 - *Format:* LP
 - Bought direct from Captured Tracks in 2017 for $25.00 (plus postage which was tolerable back then!)
