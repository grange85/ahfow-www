### My record collection [359] - Angel Corpus Christi with Dean Wareham - You / I Want Everything'

Another beautiful little 7" gem - this one is a lovely red vinyl affair with Dean Wareham guesting with Angel Corpus Christi on her own track "You" and on the flip Angel doing a wonderfully sweet take on Luna's I Want Everything.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/acc-dw-you-i-want-everything.jpg" "Angel Corpus Christi with Dean Wareham" %}

Having gushed a couple of weeks ago about [my absolute favourite 7" single in the series]() we come across a contender for that crown!

You has guitar by Dean and he's also credited as having mixed it (something that he leaves entirely to Britta these days). Dean isn't on the b-side but that doesn't make it any less perfect. The cover of I Want Everything turned up on Angel's [Bewitched: A Tribute to Luna](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2023/06/12/my-record-collection-042-angel-corpus-christi-bewitched-a-tribute-to-luna/?utm_source=social&utm_medium=substack&utm_campaign=newsletter+202606012) a couple of years back - and def worth tracking down if you can't find a copy of this beauty.

The single was released on Manchester indie Emma's House Recordings which had a short run in the early part of the century. I bought the single direct and bought a couple of other releases at the same time... maybe there was an offer?

{% ahfowvideo "OzgiwwwnMNE" "Angel Corpus Christi - I Want Everything" %}

Here's a pic of Angel Corpus Christi with Dean Wareham (and Alan Vega) - pinched without asking from Howard Thompson an age ago. By the way [Howard's Insta](https://www.instagram.com/howardthompson_/) is an absolute goldmine... as well as Dean and ACC turning up regularly it also regularly pushes my Sugarcubes and Motorhead buttons!

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/05-dean_wareham/pictures/dw_angel_vega_ht.jpg" "Dean, Angel, Alan (photo: Howard Thomspon)" %}

I think this is Angel's last appearance in this series unless I can track down [a missing single](https://www.discogs.com/release/3353457-Angel-Corpus-Christi-Dean-Wareham-Je-Taime-I-Wanna-Boogie-With-You) without having to pay twice as much again in postage!

I'd guess that Emma's House was named after the debut single by The Field Mice, released by Sarah Records in 1988.

I was lucky enough to see Deerful doing a beautiful cover of Emma's House in the church at Indietracks in 2016. I miss Indietracks!

{% ahfowvideo "vTLHFL-m71k" "Deerful - Emma's House (Indietracks, 2016)" %}

### My record collection [360] - Galaxie 500 - Peel Sessions CD

As mentioned before, back in early 2005 I had used my position of employment to acquire a copy of Galaxie 500's two Peel Sessions. Since I didn't want my employers to track the leak down to me I passed them onto Ian to share on Dimeadozen. It turns out all the cloak and dagger stuff was a little unnecessary as within six months it was announced that the Peel Sessions would be released officially. This is the second copy of the official CD release and the one that has actually been opened and played.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/galaxie-500-peel-sessions-opened-cd.jpg" "Galaxie 500 Peel Sessions CD" %}

I'm not 100% sure why I have two copies, and since I also have two copies of the poster that was limited to the first 300 copies of the disc I guess I must have ordered two at the time... which seems a little profligate of me.

The sleeve was by Lara Tomlin and rather divided fans, although the division wasn't an equal one and most, myself included, weren't that taken with it at the time. I do like it a lot more now, although not enough for the poster to find a place on display in the museum, although it didn't help that the posters were delivered folded so had a crease through Dean's face.

The CD had a hype sticker:

<blockquote>
Long awaited, definitive release of previously unavailable recordings by GALAXIE 500. Covers of the SEX PISTOLS and the YOUNG MARBLE GIANTS, together with some of the band's favorite originals.
</blockquote>

There is still no vinyl release of the Peel Sessions... just saying.
