## My record collection [371] Luna - Superfreaky Memories (CD)

Superfreaky Memories was the first single from The Days of Our Nights and was released in the UK by Beggars Banquet on 12th April 1999. I previously looked at the 7" single version.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/superfreaky-memories-cd-single.jpg" "Luna - Superfreaky Memories (CD)" %}

The CD version of the single came with one extra track - The Bad Vibe Merchant - which I think was only ever released on this single, and, with Neon Lights, as a bonus track on the Brazilian release of The Days of Our Nights.

When I first picked up Superfreaky Memories on release I was rather cool on the third track:

<blockquote>
<p>
    Well it's certainly out in the UK  I picked up a copy yesterday  and very
    good it is too  frankly if, as someone (Carsten?, Chris?) said, Superfreaky
    Memories is one of the weaker tracks on the album then we're sure in for a
    treat!
</p>

<p>
    The cover of Kraftwerk's Neon Lights is fascinating  it took me a bit of
    getting into but I do like it a lot  it's a very clever interpretation. The
    third track is a touch forgettable.
</p>
</blockquote>
<p class="caption">Wed 14 Apr 1999 17:52 ✉ Andy Aldridge</p>

Although I back-pedalled quiet considerably a couple of days later

<blockquote>
OK  I think I was a bit harsh on "The Bad Vibe Merchant" I'd still say it was
the third best track on the CD but it is still a pretty good track and it was
the one I found humming to myself around Sainsbury's this morning.
</blockquote>
<p class="caption">Thu 15 Apr 1999 17:27 ✉ Andy Aldridge</p>

And listening to it right now I can't help but thinking that I hadn't back-pedalled enough. The Bad Vibe Merchant is certainly one of the best of Luna's [lost tracks](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/category/lost-tracks/?utm_source=social&utm_medium=substack&utm_campaign=newsletter+yyyymmdd). 

Since Beggars Banquet [rather messed up the upload of the track to YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m-Dkdo6bkkQ) I've just uploaded a correction:

{% ahfowvideo "UaMq3PquXE4" "Luna - The Bad Vibe Merchant (1999)" %}

Superfreaky Memories is of course actually the best track on Days....

 - *Catalogue Number:* AHFOW 10/084
 - *Artist:* Luna
 - *Title:* Superfreaky Memories
 - *Packaging:* Digipack
 - *Format:* CD
 - Bought in April 1999, not sure where.

## My record collection [372] Street of the Love of Days - Amor de Días

While The Clientele were on hiatus Alasdair Maclean of the band released two albums of gentle folk music with  Lupe Nuñez-Fernandez (of Pipas) as Amor de Días. Damon and Naomi guested on the first of these, Street of the Love of Days, in 2011.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/amor-de-dias-street-of-the-love-of-days.jpg" "Amor de Días - Street of the Love of Days" %}

The sleeve notes list Damon and Naomi's "help" on "percussion" and "bass" but don't specify on which tracks they appear.

The album is so beautiful (as is the follow up, The House at Sea).

Amor de Días toured with Damon & Naomi quite a lot during around the time of this release and I saw them a few times because of that the first time at Cafe Oto in September 2009:

<blockquote>
Amor de Dias is the new project of Alasdair MacLean of The Clientle and Lupe Núñez-Fernández of Pipas. They played a short but very beautiful set of tropic-tinged folk guitar music accompanied on a couple of tracks by "Anne" on a huge recorder. Alasdair introduced the first track as "someone born in Aberdeen trying to play guitar like Caetano Veloso." I think a couple of the numbers were tracks from Bonfires on the Heath, the new album by The Clientele coming out next month.
</blockquote>
<p class="caption"><a href="https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2009/09/05/review-damon-naomi-cafe-oto-london/">Review: Damon & Naomi @ Cafe OTO, London</a></p>

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/amor-de-dias-cafe-oto-2009.jpg" "Amor de Días at Cafe Oto, 2009" %}

The second time was at the lovely and still missed The Luminaire in Kilburn:

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/amor-de-dias-luminaire-2010.jpg" "Amor de Días at The Luminaire, 2010" %}

While touring with Damon & Naomi the members of Amor de Días would often join them on stage. Here are both Alasdair and Lupe guesting with D&N on their cover of the Rolling Stones' Shine a Light in 2011.

{% ahfowvideo "iShJQjfpeH8" "Damon & Naomi - Shine a Light (Carrboro NC, 2011)" %}

The CLientele started making music and touring again in 2017 and have released two albums since then and an [Instagram post from a couple of weeks back](https://www.instagram.com/p/DaYq2BLDNng) showed them back in the studio "tracking strings, brass and.. ourselves"... so more coming, which is always good news.

 - *Catalogue Number:* AHFOW 10/166
 - *Artist:* Amor de Días
 - *Title:* Street of the Love of Days
 - *Format:* CD
 - Bought direct from the band for a tenner.
