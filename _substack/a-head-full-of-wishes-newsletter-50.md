### [343] Galaxie 500 - On Fire (orange vinyl LP)

Back in February 2019 Newbury Comics started releasing limited edition coloured variants of Galaxie 500's LPs - the first of these was On Fire that was released on orange/gold vinyl.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/galaxie-500-on-fire-orange-lp-newbury.jpg" "Galaxie 500 - On Fire (orange LP)" %}

Of course I already had many copies of On Fire but... I couldn't help myself and bought a copy for $25 plus $15 postage (which is twice the postage I had been charged for an LP from the US just two years previously. They then released a [maroon (with a black smudge) Today](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2023/06/19/my-record-collection-044-galaxie-500-today/?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-17) and a [Blue (with a grey smudge) This Is Our Music](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2025/01/06/my-record-collection-galaxie-500-this-is-our-music-newbury-comics-blue-swirl/?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-17)... I bought both of them.

Later came a pink On Fire and a purple This Is Our Music but I had decided to draw the line!

### [344] Decomposing the songs of Galaxie 500 and Luna

Back in 2004 some discussion on the Galaxie 500 Mailing List turned to the idea of making a fan-tribute album and I volunteered to co-ordinate the efforts. It took a couple of years since the volunteer co-ordinator was a bit rubbish, but this finally saw the light of day some time in 2006!

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/decomposing-the-songs-cdr.jpg" "Decomposing fan tribute CDR" %}

The project started with grand ambitions but eventually ended up with me burning a bunch of CDRs and laser-printing sleeves and selling them to whoever wanted a copy for $5 including postage. It deserved better. There are a bunch of lovely contributions, some faithful, some interprative, but all made by people who loved what they were doing enough to give it a go.

One of the reasons for the delay was that I wanted to be a part of it and was hoping that some talent would be bestowed upon be by some higher being. Thankfully lost are my efforts at covers of Blue Thunder and I Want Everything!

The album is available to [download here](https://static.fullofwishes.co.uk/galaxie_list_tribute/) and is worth it - it's lovely to have an album made by friends (even if the internet becoming less social means I've lost touch with most of them).

For the later AHFoW tribute albums I had invaluable help from Hannah and John and Miguel that meant they took only months instead of years to see the light of day. 
A couple of the tracks on Decomposing made it onto other releases:

- Watoo Watoo's lovely loungey Chinatown was released on their second (I think) album _La fuite_ which can be [bought on Bandcamp](https://watoowatoo.bandcamp.com/album/la-fuite).
- Ron Harrity's cover of Cold Night was included as a bonus-track on a better organised tribute album [On Fire \| 30](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2024/07/22/my-record-collection-155-on-fire-30/?utm_medium=substack&utm_source=social&utm_campaign=newsletter+2026-04-17).

The Apollo Generation, whose cover of This Time Around was included on the release, put out [an album a few years ago](https://apollo-g.bandcamp.com/album/winter-of-89) that I've only just come across and is being played right now and is sounding good!

I just noticed that the sleeve in my copy was on second-hand paper... that's how cheap/rubbish I was!

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/decomposing-the-songs-cdr-reverse.jpg" "Second-hand paper!" %}

This of course is another entry that doesn't pass the criteria for inclusion but since it was a child of the Galaxie 500 Mailing List it seemed a shame not to include it. I [wrote about the mailing list in a Substack post](https://aheadfullofwishes.substack.com/p/the-galaxie-500-mailing-list-est) last year - you can read it for free (as all my Substack posts are).

 - *Catalogue Number:* AHFOW 10/091
 - *Artist:* Various artists
 - *Title:* Decomposing the songs of Galaxie 500 and Luna
 - *Format:* CDR
 - [Download for free](https://static.fullofwishes.co.uk/galaxie_list_tribute/)
