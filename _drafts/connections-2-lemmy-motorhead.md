No, of course there is barely any connection between the AHFoW related acts and Motorhead but I thought I'd try and find a few anyway. This will, inevitably, be a post that is short on "connections" but possibly long on me rambling on about an earlier love of mine.

Back in 1979 I was fifteen and had reached the point in my life where I needed to have something of my own. For my fifteenth birthday I was given a record player. Of course the family had a _music centre_, and there were portable cassette machines we made full use of, and even a reel-to-reel 1/4" tape machine. But now there was something I could listen to on my own, in my bedroom... but what?

A lot of my collection was acquired from jumble sales, or from what I could find around the house (we had boxes and boxes of records) but I don't remember ever having a new record, of my own. Along with the record player I was also given The Story of The Who - a double album compilation. That was the beginning. It was great but it was still music imposed on me. I also got record tokens.
