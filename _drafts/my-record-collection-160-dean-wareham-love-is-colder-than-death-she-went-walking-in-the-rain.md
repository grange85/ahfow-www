Love is Colder Than Death was the first solo single since Anesthesia 22 years earlier! The a-side was a taster from the upcoming album [Emancipated Hearts](/database/dean-wareham/releases/emancipated-hearts/) while the b-side was a cover of Cheval Sombre's _She Went Walking in the Rain_.

{% ahfowimage "url" "caption" %}

The single was released in August 2013 by Sonic Cathedral
