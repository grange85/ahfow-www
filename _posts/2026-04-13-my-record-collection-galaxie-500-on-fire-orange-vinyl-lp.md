Back in February 2019 Newbury Comics started releasing limited edition coloured variants of Galaxie 500's LPs - the first of these was On Fire that was released on orange/gold vinyl.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/galaxie-500-on-fire-orange-lp-newbury.jpg" "Galaxie 500 - On Fire (orange LP)" %}

Of course I already had many copies of On Fire but... I couldn't help myself and bought a copy for $25 plus $15 postage (which is twice the postage I had been charged for an LP from the US just two years previously. They then released a [maroon (with a black smudge) Today](/2023/06/19/my-record-collection-044-galaxie-500-today/) and a [Blue (with a grey smudge) This Is Our Music](/2025/01/06/my-record-collection-galaxie-500-this-is-our-music-newbury-comics-blue-swirl/)... I bought both of them.

Later came a pink On Fire and a purple This Is Our Music but I had decided to draw the line!

 - *Catalogue Number:* AHFOW 12/042
 - *Artist:* Galaxie 500
 - *Title:* On Fire
 - *Notes:* Orange vinyl
 - *Format:* LP
 - Bought from Newbury Comics for $24.99 plus postage.
