<p>The two very excellent Galaxie 500 sessions recorded for BBC Radio 1's legendary John Peel show in 1989 and 1990 are getting a long overdue release on 14 November.</p>
<p>The sessions are being released on Damon & Naomi's <a href="http://www.20-20-20.com/">20/20/20</a> label on 14 November 2005 - the first 300 orders will also receive a poster. You can pre-order the album from <span class="removed_link" title="http://www.damonandnaomi.com/about/g500.html">damonandnaomi.com</span></p>
