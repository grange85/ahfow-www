This was the first vinyl release of Dean & Britta's first album released in October 2008.

{% ahfowimage "url" "caption" %}

While having a look through AHFoW to find out when this was released I found [a news item](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2008/07/23/the-lavventura-vinyl-re-release-will-have-a-free-cd/) that declared that this vinyl release would come with a CD copy of the album and I think this explains how the [second promo CD](https://www.fullofwishes.co.uk/2025/01/09/my-record-collection-dean-britta-l-avventura-cd-promo/) came into my possession!
