Damon will be [hosting a Bandcamp listening party](https://damonandnaomi.bandcamp.com/merch/whats-past-is-prologue-live-at-caf-oto-listening-party) for _What's Past is Prologue_, Damon & Naomi's live album of Galaxie 500 covers. The party will be on Friday 17th (tomorrow) at 12:30pm Pacific, 3:30pm Eastern or  8:30pm BST.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/03-damon_and_naomi/sleeves/damon-and-naomi-whats-past-is-prologue.jpg" "What's Past is Prologue" %}

The album was recorded over three wonderful nights at Cafe Oto in London and featured a number of guest appearances.

Here's Damon's invite:

<blockquote>
I'm going to host a Listening Party of our Bandcamp-exclusive release What's Past is Prologue on <strong>Friday April 17 at 12:30pm Pacific / 3:30pm Eastern / 8:30pm UK time</strong>. Come listen to the album with me and chat about it - this is a live recording of Galaxie 500 songs Naomi and I played as encores during our recent residency at Café Oto in London, with friends Laetitia Sadier, Mark Webber, Richard Youngs, Jess Hickie-Kallenbach, Alabaster DePlume, Gina Birch, Marie Merlet and Stewart Lee. There's lots to discuss! 
</blockquote>
<p class="caption">Damon Krukowski on Bandcamp</p>
