<a href="/database/track/dance-with-me/">Dance With Me</a> was recorded by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Modern_Lovers">The Modern Lovers</a> in the 1972 sessions with producer <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kim_Fowley">Kim Fowley</a> but not released until the Original Modern Lovers LP was released in 1981.</p>
{% ahfowvideo "odzLFt6YIuc" "" %}
<p>Luna's version was recorded during the sessions for Penthouse and first appeared as the flip of the <a href="http://db.fullofwishes.co.uk/database/discography/luna/262">Bobby Peru 7"</a> and later on the <a href="http://db.fullofwishes.co.uk/database/discography/luna/64">split 7" single with (The Feelies off-shoot) Wake Ooloo</a>.</p>
