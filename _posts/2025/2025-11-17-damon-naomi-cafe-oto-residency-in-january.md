Damon & Naomi will be coming to London in the new year to play a three night residency at the lovely Cafe Oto in Dalston.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/03-damon_and_naomi/pictures/damon-and-naomi-cafe-oto-2026.jpg" "Damon & Naomi Cafe Oto poster" %}

They play the 3rd, 4th, and 5th of January and will have "friends" guesting - including Stewart Lee, Laetitia Sadier, Richard Youngs and Gina Birch, "and more tba!"

[Tickets are available now](https://www.cafeoto.co.uk/events/damon-naomi-three-day-residency/) - either for individual shows or a discounted three day pass.
