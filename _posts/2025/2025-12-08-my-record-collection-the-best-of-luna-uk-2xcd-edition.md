While the US got a single disc Best of Luna and Lunafied as a download, the bands UK label, Beggars Banquet gave us on this side of the pond a lovely two disc edition with a whole disc of _Lunafied_ cover versions.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/best-of-luna-uk-cd-playing.jpg" "Best of Luna (UK)" %}

The discs were named "Best of Luna" and "Lunafied" although this led to the awkwardness of having a "Best of Luna" disc without any cover versions - this could almost be fixed by the fact that the whole package was called "Best of Luna" but then... should a "Best of Luna" package have "Only Women Bleed" or "In The Flesh"?

When I covered the US "Best of Luna" I created a table stating the album origins of each of the tracks... so, lets revisit and compare:

| Best of Luna           | US | UK 
|------------------------|----|----
| Lunapark               |3   |2
| Bewitched              |4   |4
| Penthouse              |5   |5
| Pup Tent               |2   |2
| The Days of Our Nights |1   |2
| Romantica              |0   |2
| Rendezvous             |1   |0 
| non album track        |0   |1

So once again The Best of Luna is heavy on their first three albums which makes up almost two thirds of the album. And Rendezvous doesn't get a look in! The album that Dean put at number 2 when he [ranked Luna's studio albums](/2016/05/12/dean-wareham-ranks-lunas-studio-albums/) back in 2016!

I tried to think of the 18 songs I'd put on **my** Best of Luna but I'm damned if I could!

The liner notes for Best of Luna had short little pen portraits of each song on the album... although they're often only superficially about the song... here are a couple of my favourites.

I found that quite interesting because Dean's version seems to be like one of Adrian Tomine's most famous New Yorker covers... also on the front cover of his New York Drawings, here's my copy:

I love Adrian Tomine's art, and did before it turned up on a Luna album... I can't really remember how I learnt about him - although possibly through someone on the Galaxie 500 Mailing List.

Lunafied also gets little one-sentence sketches...

 - Outdoor Miner - _We recorded this for a 7" single on a Spanish label, "Why don't you choose the artwork?" I told them. "I'm sure it'll be fine". They chose a gruesome photograph of a dead cat with a metal stake driven through its eye._
 - Only Women Bleed - _... Justin refused to play on this ballad, so we waited till he was out of the band - it was recorded during the making of Romantica._
 - Bonnie and  Clyde - _We couldn't decide if "Bonnie and Clyde" worked better fast or slow, so we did it both ways._
