So another duplicate in my _one for show, one for blow_ collection... although I guess since I'm playing everything in this series... they're now both _for blow_? Love is Colder Than Death was Dean's first solo single after more than a 20 year gap and was released on Sonic Cathedral in 2013.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/dean-wareham-love-is-colder-than-death-2.jpg" "Dean Wareham - Love is Colder Than Death" %}

Given that I've already covered the single twice - one the same as this, and the test pressing that Nat sent me back then - I guess there's not a lot more to say... maybe I'll just give a nod to another classic Dean rhyme - "cats and mices have their vices".

This is one of two copies I bought when Sonic Cathedral were selling them for just two quid a pop in 2014. I think I gave one away in a competition, or as a survey incentive, at some point.

Here's a nice clip of Dean, Britta and Roger playing the song in 2018.
