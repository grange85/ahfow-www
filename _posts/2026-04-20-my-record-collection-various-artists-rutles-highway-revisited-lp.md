My previous post for the [CD version of the fabulous Rutles Highway Revisited](/2025/05/29/my-record-collection-rutles-highway-revisited-cd/) was mostly a love-letter to the late and still much missed Neil Innes so I'll try and keep _on topic_ this time.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/rutles-highway-revisited-lp.jpg" "Rutles Highway Revisited (LP)" %}

I do love most of the tracks on this album but, like The Beatles, The Rutles were better when they were being sweet, so it's the sweet songs that work better for me. So... here are what are probably my favourite three tracks on the album (that aren't Galaxie 500), although that does involve leaving a lot of ones I also love off the list.

{% ahfowvideo "PNAD7XOfb5o" "The Pussywillows - Hold My Hand" "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/rutles-tribute-the-pussywillows.jpg" %}

_pic lifted from [this page with a 1990 session the duo played for KCRW](https://www.kcrw.com/shows/bent-by-nature/stories/syd-straw-marc-ribot-1990-session) which opens with a lovely version of I Must Be in Love._
