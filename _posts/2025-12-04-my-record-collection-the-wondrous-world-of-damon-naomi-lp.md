This is the Sub Pop LP release of Damon & Naomi's second album The Wondrous World of Damon & Naomi. I originally bought this on CD around the time of release and bought this LP in 2013 on Discogs for the collection.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/damon-and-naomi-wondrous-world-og-lp.jpg" "The Wondrous World of Damon & Naomi (LP)" %}

I loved Wondrous World on release and never stopped loving it (which is I think why I loved [the "bootleg" edition](/2025/06/23/my-record-collection-the-wondrous-world-of-damon-naomi-bootleg-edition/) slightly less).

I know I've said in the last couple of posts about Damon & Naomi albums that I find it impossible to pick a favourite but, for the annual AHFoW survey that ran from 1996 until 2020, I was obliged to pick an album. For most of those years it was _Wondrous World..._... except for the years it was _... with Ghost_.

While it is lovely to have the album in a 12" package it doesn't have the beautiful collage artwork that Naomi used for the CD booklet... so, swings and roundabouts.
