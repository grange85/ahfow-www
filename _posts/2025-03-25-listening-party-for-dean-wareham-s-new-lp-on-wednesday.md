Carpark Records will be hosting a [listening party](https://deanwareham.bandcamp.com/merch/thats-the-price-of-loving-me-listening-party) for Dean Wareham's new album _That's the Price of Loving Me_. Dean will be "in the chat room and will add some notes about the songs and try to answer questions" - the party will be hosted on Bandcamp on Wednesday (26th March) at noon Pacific time, which is 3pm Eastern, and 7pm in the UK - if you go to the [Bandcamp page](https://deanwareham.bandcamp.com/merch/thats-the-price-of-loving-me-listening-party) it should be able to tell you the time wherever you are.
