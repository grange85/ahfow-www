This Cheval Sombre single was released in 2008 by Static Caravan and contains I Found It Not So on the a-side, produced by Sonic Boom and with instrumental contributions from both Dean and Britta. Britta also mixed it.

{% ahfowimage "url" "caption" %}

The b-side has a cover of The Supremes' Where Did Our Love Go.
