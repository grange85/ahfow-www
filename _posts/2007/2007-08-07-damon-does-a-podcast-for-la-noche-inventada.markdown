<p>Damon Krukowski has put together an hour long podcast of some other people's "sad songs" for Spanish radio show La Noche Inventada as well as tracks by the artists on International Sad Hits (volume 1) you can also here some fine tracks by Sandy Denny, Robert Wyatt, Tim Buckley and Pentangle amongst many others.</p>
<p>Well worth a listen and worth keeping an eye on <a href="http://lanocheinventada.podomatic.com">La Noche Inventada</a> as they have some other interesting guest DJs in the coming weeks.</p>
