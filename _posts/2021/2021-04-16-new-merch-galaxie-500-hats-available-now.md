How can you show your love for Galaxie 500 stylishly while you're wearing your suit? Well, now available from [Damon & Naomi's online store](https://www.20-20-20.com/store/) are some lovely hats with embroidered Galaxie 500 patches.

Available in two designs, either a [blue 'beanie'](https://www.20-20-20.com/store/galaxie-500-hat-beanie) or a [grey 'dad' cap](https://www.20-20-20.com/store/galaxie-500-hat-6panel) and available now for $20 a pop.

Nope... until right now I'd never even heard of a 'dad' cap!
