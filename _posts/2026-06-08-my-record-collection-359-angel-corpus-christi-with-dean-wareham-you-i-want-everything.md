Another beautiful little 7" gem - this one is a lovely red vinyl affair with Dean Wareham guesting with Angel Corpus Christi on her own track "You" and on the flip Angel doing a wonderfully sweet take on Luna's I Want Everything.

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/00-misc/my-record-collection/acc-dw-you-i-want-everything.jpg" "Angel Corpus Christi with Dean Wareham" %}

Having gushed a couple of weeks ago about [my absolute favourite 7" single in the series]() we come across a contender for that crown!

You has guitar by Dean and he's also credited as having mixed it (something that he leaves entirely to Britta these days). Dean isn't on the b-side but that doesn't make it any less perfect. The cover of I Want Everything turned up on Angel's [Bewitched: A Tribute to Luna](/2023/06/12/my-record-collection-042-angel-corpus-christi-bewitched-a-tribute-to-luna/) a couple of years back - and def worth tracking down if you can't find a copy of this beauty.

The single was released on Manchester indie Emma's House Recordings which had a short run in the early part of the century. I bought the single direct and bought a couple of other releases at the same time... maybe there was an offer?

{% ahfowvideo "OzgiwwwnMNE" "Angel Corpus Christi - I Want Everything" %}

Here's a pic of Angel Corpus Christi with Dean Wareham (and Alan Vega) - pinched without asking from Howard Thompson an age ago. By the way [Howard's Insta](https://www.instagram.com/howardthompson_/) is an absolute goldmine... as well as Dean and ACC turning up regularly it also regularly pushes my Sugarcubes and Motorhead buttons!

{% ahfowimage "https://media.fullofwishes.co.uk/05-dean_wareham/pictures/dw_angel_vega_ht.jpg" "Dean, Angel, Alan (photo: Howard Thomspon)" %}

I think this is Angel's last appearance in this series unless I can track down [a missing single](https://www.discogs.com/release/3353457-Angel-Corpus-Christi-Dean-Wareham-Je-Taime-I-Wanna-Boogie-With-You) without having to pay twice as much again in postage!

I'd guess that Emma's House was named after the debut single by The Field Mice, released by Sarah Records in 1988.

I was lucky enough to see Deerful doing a beautiful cover of Emma's House in the church at Indietracks in 2016. I miss Indietracks!
